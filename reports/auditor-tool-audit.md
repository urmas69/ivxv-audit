# Auditor tool audit

`auditor` tools are registered in the auditor application and consume ballot-box, shuffle/decryption proofs, logs and tally structures. `DecryptTool.java:107-179` checks that proof ciphertexts have counterparts and removes checked entries from its working copy; comments at `319-324` and membership checks at `547-571` make the mutation and correspondence assumptions explicit. These checks rely on externally supplied manifests, proof files and configuration authenticity. No confirmed bypass was found; malformed-proof, duplicate-proof, cross-election and stale-input tests remain required because the publication lacks complete election fixtures.
